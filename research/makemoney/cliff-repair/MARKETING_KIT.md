## Quick Tips

1. **Respond FAST** - First to respond usually gets the job
2. **Be specific about availability** - "I can come Tuesday at 2pm or Thursday morning"
3. **Text a confirmation** - Reduces no-shows
4. **Take before/after photos** - Great for future marketing
5. **Ask for reviews** - "If you're happy, would you mind leaving a quick review?"
6. **Collect emails** - For seasonal reminders later
