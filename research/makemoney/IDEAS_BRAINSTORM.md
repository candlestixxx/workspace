**Viability:** ⭐⭐⭐

|--------|------------|
| Startup Cost | $50-100 |
| Conversion Rate | 1-3% typical |
| Risk Level | Low-Medium |

### Kratom Extract / 7-OH Pills
**Status:** ❌ DO NOT PURSUE

| Risk | Severity |
|------|----------|
| Legal | HIGH - 7-OH specifically being banned in many states |
| FDA | Active enforcement against kratom sellers |
| Liability | Extreme - people have died, lawsuits ongoing |
| Payment | Banks/processors blacklist kratom businesses |

**Why this is dangerous:**
- 7-hydroxymitragynine (7-OH) is being specifically targeted by regulators
- Several states have banned it in 2024-2025
- FDA has issued warnings and seized products
- People have died from concentrated extracts
- You could face criminal charges

**The "popular right now" aspect is exactly WHY regulators are cracking down.**
