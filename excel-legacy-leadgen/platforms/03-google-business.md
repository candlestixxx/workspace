## REVIEW GENERATION STRATEGY
### Target: 50+ reviews at 4.9+ stars within 6 months

### Process
1. After every closing, send review request email/text within 24 hours
2. Include direct Google review link
3. Follow up at 3 days if no response
4. Thank every reviewer publicly within 24 hours

### Review Request Template
"Hi [Name]! It was a pleasure helping you [buy/sell] your home! If you had a great experience, we'd be so grateful if you could leave us a quick Google review. It helps other families find us. Here's the direct link: [link]. Thank you! - The Excel Legacy Team"

### Review Response Templates
**5-Star:** "Thank you so much, [Name]! It was a pleasure working with you. We're thrilled we could help you [specific detail]. Welcome to your new home / congrats on the sale! - The Excel Legacy Team"

**4-Star:** "Thank you for the review, [Name]! We're glad we could help. We'd love to hear how we could make it a 5-star experience next time. Feel free to reach out anytime!"

**Negative:** "We're sorry to hear about your experience, [Name]. We take feedback seriously and would like to make this right. Please contact us directly at [phone/email] so we can discuss this personally."
