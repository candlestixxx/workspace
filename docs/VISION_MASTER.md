# The Global Vision (Monorepo Master Plan)

## 1. The Core Philosophy: "Proof of Health"
- **Proof of Dance:** Arcade machines and exercise equipment act as "Mining Oracles."
- **High-Velocity Economy:** Anti-hoarding design to keep tokens flowing through the community.
- **Physical/Digital Bridge:** Real-world activity fuels digital value and gaming experiences.

## 2. The Infrastructure: Rhythm Games & Beyond
We maintain and evolve the definitive rhythm game ecosystem.
- **ITGMania (Stable):** The tournament-grade fork based on StepMania 3.95. Focus on stability, mods, and cabinet performance.
- **Unified Tooling:** Integrated support for **ArrowVortex** (editing), **DDC** (auto-charting), and **FFR Difficulty Calculation**.

## 3. The Brain: aios (Meta-Orchestrator)
The `aios/` project is the central nervous system of the workspace.
- **Universal Hub:** Orchestrates tools, agents, and MCP servers across all projects.
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Manages cognitive load by loading tools/context on-demand.
- **Agent Economy:** Future integration of autonomous agents working to maintain, develop, and play within the ecosystem.

## 4. Current Strategic Goals (Monorepo Keeper)
- **Consolidation:** Move all project-specific work into high-quality submodules.
- **Root-Level Leaness:** The workspace root is an orchestration layer, not a dumping ground.
- **AI Integration:** Every project (Fwber, Hellven, okgame) will eventually be reachable and improvable via the `aios` hub.
