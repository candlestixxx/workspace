General Information

Work Title Amazing Grace
Alternative. Title New Britain ; Harmony Grove
Composer Anonymous
Internal Reference NumberInternal Ref. No. IA 31
Key C major
Text Incipit Amazing grace! How sweet the sound
First Publication. 1835 in The Southern Harmony
Librettist John Newton (1725–1807)
Language English
Piece Style Traditional (folk)
Instrumentation 3-part chorus (SSB or TTB)
Related Works Pieces based on Amazing Grace
External Links Wikipedia article
The Southern Harmony, 1854 ed. (p.8)
Extra Information N.B. This page refers only to the familiar New Britain melody, not to other settings of Newton's text.
Walker's melody is an amalgamation of two other hymn melodies ("Gallaher" and "St. Mary"), first published in the Columbian Harmony by Charles H. Spilman and Benjamin Shaw (Cincinnati, 1829)
